CS180 — Project 0: Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Jefferson Li

Overview

This project explores how camera distance and focal length change the pictures. In Part 1, I compare a close, wide-angle selfie to a farther, longer-focal-length shot to show facial distortion vs. a more natural look. In Part 2, I demonstrate architectural perspective compression at two locations by stepping back and zooming in. Part 3 presents a dolly zoom that keeps subject size constant while the background appears to stretch and compress.
